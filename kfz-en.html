A motor vehicle appraiser not only assesses the damage caused in a car accident, but also the value of your vehicle before and after the accident. The costs for the motor vehicle expert are settled with the insurance company in the event of a traffic accident through no fault of your own

Advantages of a motor vehicle appraiser at a glance

Advantages of a motor vehicle appraiser at a glance

  • As an accident victim, you are always entitled to an independent motor vehicle appraiser. In the event of a traffic accident through no fault of your own, the opposing insurance company must pay for the costs
  • We make sure that your right to a neutral vehicle expert is actually implemented and will provide you with an expert for the accident report
  • An independent vehicle appraiser is important because he calculates the damage neutrally. In addition, issues such as depreciation and other damage items are included in the calculation.
  • This service is free of charge for you as an accident victim, because the other party's insurance company will pay for it.
    • This applies to all of our other services for claims settlement from a single source : arranging a lawyer, a replacement vehicle, a repair workshop and all other costs incurred as a result of the accident damage.
